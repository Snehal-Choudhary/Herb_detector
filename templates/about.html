🌿 About Plant Identifier

This application helps you identify plants using the Pl@ntNet API. Simply capture an image of a plant, and our system will identify it for you.

📜 Features:

  • Instant camera capture & plant identification
  • Shows common & scientific names
  • Displays Ayurvedic uses of plants

👨‍💻 Developer Info

Developed using Flask, OpenCV, and JavaScript.
